Detailed Description

Comparative metrics between two selected frequency ranges.

Attributes: left: Left range name. right: Right range name. area_ratio: Ratio of areas left/right. area_difference: Difference of areas left-right. peak_ratio: Ratio of peak amplitudes. centroid_shift: Difference of centroids left-right. correlation: Pearson correlation of resampled spectral shapes. kl_divergence: KL divergence between normalized spectral shapes. cosine_similarity: Cosine similarity between spectral shapes.
