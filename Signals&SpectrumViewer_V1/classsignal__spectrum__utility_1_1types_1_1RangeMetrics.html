Detailed Description

Absolute metrics for one selected frequency range.

Attributes: area: Area under spectrum curve. peak_frequency: Frequency of spectrum maximum. peak_amplitude: Maximum amplitude. mean_amplitude: Mean amplitude in the range. bandwidth_3db: Bandwidth at -3dB level relative to the peak. centroid: Spectral centroid. rms: Root mean square amplitude. spectral_flatness: Flatness of spectral distribution. spectral_rolloff: Frequency below which 85%% of range energy is accumulated.
