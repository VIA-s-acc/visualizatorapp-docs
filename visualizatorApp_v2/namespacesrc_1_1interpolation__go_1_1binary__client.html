Calculate adaptive timeout based on grid size.

Parameters
grid_sizeTotal number of grid points (nx * ny * nz)
base_timeoutBase timeout in seconds
Returns
Adaptive timeout in seconds

Timeout scaling:

  • < 1M points: base_timeout (120s)
  • 1M - 10M points: scale linearly up to 5 * base_timeout (600s)
  • 10M - 50M points: scale linearly up to 10 * base_timeout (1200s)
  • > 50M points: 10 * base_timeout (1200s max)
