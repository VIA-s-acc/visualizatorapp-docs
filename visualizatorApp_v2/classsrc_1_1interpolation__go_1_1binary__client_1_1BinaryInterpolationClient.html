Detailed Description

Fast binary client for interpolation service.

Protocol format: Request: [ver(1)][method_len(1)][method][n_points(4)][dims...][data...] Response: [shape_len(1)][shape...][n_values(4)][values_raw...]

Optimizations:

  • Large socket buffers (4MB send/recv)
  • 1MB recv chunks (vs 65KB original)
  • bytearray + recv_into for recv (no O(n^2) concatenation)
  • TCP_NODELAY to disable Nagle
